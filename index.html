About Our School

Our history and philosophy

Founded in 2005, Ananda School Bekasi has established itself as a leading educational institution in the region, combining academic excellence with character development.

Our holistic approach to education ensures that students not only excel academically but also develop strong moral values, creativity, and critical thinking skills.

  • Certified and experienced teachers
  • Modern teaching methods
  • Character building programs
  • Safe and nurturing environment
